Music Trainer!

Your ears and your brain are the instrument.

Project is under development. Basic interface in place, Melody and Tempo Trainer are fully functional. Music Generator is an on-going experiment, with a visualizer and clean interface coming soon. This project was a learning exercise, and needs a full refactor to a state-less object-oriented architecture. However, the solutions to the 'musical problems' are valid, and produce working results.
